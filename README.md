üìò Introduction

The electric vehicle (EV) industry is undergoing rapid transformation driven by sustainability goals, technological advancements, and government incentives. Understanding EV adoption patterns, market composition, and performance metrics is essential for policymakers, automotive manufacturers, and strategic decision-makers.
This Tableau project analyzes electric vehicle data to provide a clear, structured, and insight-driven view of the EV market, covering both Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs). The dashboard is designed to support business and policy decisions through intuitive visual storytelling.

üéØ Problem Statement

As the EV market expands, stakeholders face challenges in understanding:
-The true size and growth trajectory of the EV market.
-The adoption split between fully electric and hybrid models.
-Technological progress in electric range.
-Regional differences in EV adoption.
-Manufacturer and model-level market dominance.
-The role of clean fuel incentives in driving adoption.
-Without a consolidated analytical view, it becomes difficult to make informed strategic decisions.

üß† Objectives

This project aims to:
-Analyze the total number of electric vehicles in the market.
-Determine the average electric range to assess technological efficiency.
-Identify the total BEV count and its percentage share in the EV market.
-Identify the total PHEV count and its percentage share in the EV market.
-Track EV adoption trends over time (from 2010 onwards).
-Examine state-wise EV distribution.
-Identify top manufacturers and models by total vehicles.
-Evaluate CAFV eligibility and its impact on EV adoption.

üìÇ Dataset Description

The dataset consists of electric vehicle registration and performance data, including:
-Vehicle type (BEV / PHEV)
-Model year
-Manufacturer and model
-Electric range
-State-wise distribution
-CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility
-The data has been cleaned and structured to ensure consistency and analytical accuracy.

üìä Dashboard & Visualizations

-EV Growth Over Time (2010 onwards)
Line / Area chart illustrating year-wise growth in electric vehicle adoption, helping identify long-term market trends and acceleration phases.
-State-wise Electric Vehicle Distribution
Map chart highlighting geographical adoption patterns and identifying states with higher EV penetration.
-Top 10 Electric Vehicle Manufacturers
Bar chart showcasing leading manufacturers based on total number of registered electric vehicles, reflecting brand dominance in the market.

Pie / Donut chart representing the proportion of vehicles eligible for clean fuel incentives, helping assess the impact of policy support on adoption.

üîç Key Insights

-The EV market shows strong year-on-year growth, particularly after 2015.
-BEVs dominate the EV landscape, indicating a shift toward fully electric mobility.
-Average electric range has steadily improved, reflecting technological progress.
-EV adoption is concentrated in specific states, acting as early adopters.
-A limited number of manufacturers and models control a significant market share.
-CAFV eligibility appears to positively influence EV adoption rates.

üí° Business Recommendations

-Expand charging infrastructure in high-adoption and emerging regions.
-Encourage adoption in low-penetration states through policy incentives.
-Manufacturers should focus on range efficiency and cost optimization.
-Policymakers can leverage CAFV insights to refine incentive programs.

Limitations
-The analysis is limited to the variables available in the dataset and does not account for factors such as pricing, charging infrastructure density, or consumer income levels.

‚úÖ Conclusion

This Electric Vehicle analysis provides a comprehensive view of the evolving EV ecosystem by combining market size, technology trends, regional adoption, and manufacturer performance into a single, interactive Tableau dashboard.

Overall, this project demonstrates how data visualization and analytical thinking can transform complex datasets into clear, actionable insights for business and policy decision-making.
